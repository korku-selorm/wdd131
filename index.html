Dansoman, Ghana

Dansoman, located in Accra, Ghana, is one of the largest planned residential estates in the country and is often called the city of entertainment due to its vibrant music and cultural scene. It is home to well-knownschools such as St. Margaret Mary and Ebenezer Senior High, as well as Liberty Professionals Football Club, which contributes to its strong sports culture. The community is also a food lover's destination, with numerous spots serving traditional dishes like waakye and kenkey. Developed in the late 1960s, Dansoman has grown into a lively neighborhood that blends modern living with rich Ghanaian culture.
